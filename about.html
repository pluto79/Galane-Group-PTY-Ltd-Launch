About Us

Who we are?

Galane Group (Pty) Ltd is a premier provider of integrated solutions in landscaping, cleaning materials, and cleaning equipment. Founded in 2020, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality products and services that enhance the aesthetics, functionality, and cleanliness of residential, commercial, and industrial environments. With a strong emphasis on sustainability and innovation, Galane Group (Pty) Ltd strives to create healthier and more beautiful spaces for our clients. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and products that meet the highest standards of quality, efficiency, and environmental responsibility

When was the Galane Group (Pty) Ltd founded?

Galane Group (Pty) Ltd was established 22 December 2020 by business partners and brothers, Jones Galane and Kapei Galane.
